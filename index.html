SHC is a for women, by women digital security clinic supporting survivors of technology-facilitated gender-based violence. Have you experienced any form of violence through technology Do you think someone is using technology to control, monitor, or harm you? Talk to us

You Are SEEN

Every case is unique, and we take the time to understand the specific ways technology has been weaponized against you—whether it's stalkerware tracking your every move, an ex-partner accessing your bank accounts, or deepfake images. You're not paranoid. You're not overreacting. If you feel watched, monitored, or violated, we believe you.

You Are GUIDED

We walk beside you, not ahead of you. Tech-enabled abuse can feel overwhelming and impossible to navigate alone. Between unfamiliar technical terms, complex security processes, and the emotional weight of confronting your abuse, it's easy to feel lost. That's where we come in. We create a safe space where you can share your story at your own pace, without fear of being dismissed, blamed, or misunderstood.

You Are PROTECTED

Your safety—digital, physical, and emotional—is our highest priority. From the moment you reach out to us through your final security check-in, we create a personalized roadmap based on your unique situation, threat level, and goals. You'll always know what to expect next. we equip you with the knowledge and tools to maintain your security long after our work together ends. You'll learn how to spot warning signs, secure your devices, and protect yourself moving forward.

We believe you have the right to both safety & connection.

SHC is built on the principles of a feminist internet . We’re here to help women through every step of reclaiming digital safety and freedom.

Our Commitment to You

When you work with Safe Her Clinic, you can expect:

  • Trauma-informed care at every touchpoint.
    Every member of our team is trained in trauma-informed approaches.

  • Transparency and informed consent.
    You'll understand exactly what we're doing, why we're doing it, and what your options are at every stage. Your autonomy matters to us. We guarantee the confidentiality of both the data and the stories shared with us.

  • Collaboration, not prescription.
    This is your journey. We provide expert recommendations, but you make the final decisions about your case. We respect your agency completely.

  • Cultural sensitivity and inclusivity.
    We recognize that survivors come from diverse backgrounds with unique needs, cultural contexts, and safety considerations. We tailor our approach accordingly.

  • Accountability and quality control.
    Every case undergoes rigorous quality review. Our Quality Control Officers ensure that our work meets the highest standards and that you receive the comprehensive support you deserve.
